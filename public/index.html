Bella Bakes for you

Bella Bakes is your cozy neighborhood bakery and patisserie known for our delectable pastries and fresh bread. Bella lovingly crafts treats that warm the heart and delight the taste buds.

Baked Goods

Fresh Bread

Bella takes the art of bread-making to heart. Her freshly baked bread, including the beloved sourdough, is a labor of love. Each loaf is handcrafted with the finest ingredients and allowed to ferment slowly, resulting in a crispy crust and a soft, airy interior. Whether you're enjoying it toasted with butter or as the foundation for your favorite sandwich, her bread will transport your taste buds to a world of rustic, wholesome flavors.

French Pastries

Indulge in the exquisite flavors of France with Bella's delectable French pastries. Her flaky, buttery croissants are a true delight, with each layer carefully folded to perfection. These pastries are an ode to tradition, and they come in various flavors, including classic butter, chocolate, and almond. Sweet Treats

Bella believes that every day is a good day for something sweet. Her collection of sweet treats, including pies, tarts, and macarons, is designed to satisfy your cravings and bring joy to your taste buds. Her pies boast flaky, homemade crusts filled with luscious fruit or velvety custard. Her tarts are works of art, featuring vibrant seasonal fruits atop delicate pastry - a symphony of flavor in every bite. Each sweet treat is a testament to Bella's commitment to quality and taste, making every bite a memorable experience.
